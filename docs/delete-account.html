Account & Data Deletion Request

You can request permanent deletion of your PLOS account and all data associated with it at any time. Fill out the short form below and tap Submit Request. Your email app will open with a pre-filled message addressed to our support team.

What will be deleted

  • Your account credentials (email, hashed password)
  • Your profile (name, address, date of birth)
  • All financial data \u2014 income, expenses, debts, investments, transactions
  • All connected bank accounts and Plaid access tokens
  • All career data \u2014 resumes, job applications, tailored packages
  • All health, safety, and family data
  • All AI conversation history
  • Push notification tokens and device identifiers
This action is permanent. Once processed, your data cannot be recovered. Requests are typically completed within 7 business days.

Prefer to email directly?

Send a message to plos.support@gmail.com with the subject PLOS Account Deletion Request and include your account email address and reason. We will process the deletion within 7 business days and email you when it is complete.
