# PLOS — Google Play Content Rating Questionnaire

**Recommended target rating:** Teen (13+). Google will likely assign Teen due
to Financial services + Location sharing. If the Play Console prompts you to
submit as 17+/Mature, choose Teen — PLOS has no mature themes.
